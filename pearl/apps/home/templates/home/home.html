About CtoG

CtoG is a Human Genome Analysis, Interpretation & Reporting platform. CtoG uses an intuitive ranking system to identify clinically relevant variants. CtoG annotates variants from multiple knowledge bases including Leucine Rich Bioâ€™s Human Genome Variation Database (HGVD).

Along with bioinformatics reports, CtoG outputs clinical reports which are customized for clinicians. The clinical report will contain relevant information that is needed to make clinical decisions. Clinical reports are tailored to provide highly ranked disease-causing or clinically pathogenic variants. The report will also contain variants related to pharmacogenomics.

CtoG is still in its beta version. More updates will be added soon to make it a robust and consistent across all platforms. CtoG is being tested for HIPAA compliance. Please use CtoG for research data only, till all the compliances are met.

Variant Calling

CtoG uses Bowtie2 and Platypus to call variants. CtoG additionally uses its own proprietary algorithms to add an additional value to variant calling.

Variant Annotation

CtoG annotates variants from multiple publically available knowledge bases. CtoG also annotates variants from HGVD, an in-house database which contains manually curated variant information from scientific literature.

Variant Ranking

Using proprietary ranking algorithm, CtoG ranks variants for clinical significance. Ranking is based on multiple factors, including curated literature, allele frequency and various bioinformatics scores. Ranking allows for easy identification of pathogenic variants.
